# European Retail Revenue Intelligence & Profit Leakage Audit

## Executive Summary
This project delivers an end-to-end retail revenue analytics solution designed to monitor financial performance, detect profit leakage, and support strategic decision-making. The solution integrates Excel, MySQL, and Power BI to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence.

---

## Business Problem
Retail organizations often struggle with:
- Inconsistent revenue tracking across regions
- Limited visibility into profit margins
- Undetected profit leakage due to discounts, returns, or operational inefficiencies
- Lack of centralized performance dashboards

The objective was to design a structured analytics framework to improve revenue visibility and profitability analysis.

---

## Objectives
- Consolidate retail sales data into a structured analytical model
- Identify revenue trends across regions and product categories
- Analyze profit margins and detect leakage areas
- Build an executive dashboard for performance monitoring

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Initial Data Cleaning & Structuring)
- MySQL (Data Querying & Aggregation)
- Power BI (Data Modeling & Dashboard Development)
- DAX (KPI & Calculated Measures)

---

## Data Processing Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning (Excel)
- Removed duplicate records
- Standardized date and currency formats
- Validated missing and inconsistent values

### 2. Data Extraction & Analysis (MySQL)
- Wrote SQL queries to:
  - Aggregate revenue by region and category
  - Calculate profit margins
  - Identify high-discount transactions
  - Detect anomalies in sales patterns
- Created grouped summaries for dashboard integration

### 3. Data Modeling (Power BI)
- Built relational data model
- Created calculated measures:
  - Total Revenue
  - Gross Profit
  - Profit Margin %
  - Year-over-Year Growth
- Applied filter context and slicers for interactive exploration

---

## Dashboard Design Strategy
The dashboard was structured into:

- KPI Summary Section (Revenue, Profit, Margin %)
- Regional Performance Analysis
- Category-wise Contribution
- Time-Series Revenue Trends
- Profit Leakage Identification Metrics

Design focus:
- Executive-level clarity
- Interactive drill-down capability
- Clean visual hierarchy

---

## Key Insights Generated
- Identified top-performing regions contributing maximum revenue.
- Detected profit leakage due to excessive discounting in specific categories.
- Observed seasonal revenue patterns affecting overall profitability.
- Highlighted low-margin product segments requiring strategic intervention.

---

## Business Impact
This solution enables:
- Data-driven pricing decisions
- Margin optimization strategies
- Real-time revenue monitoring
- Improved financial transparency

The dashboard framework can be scaled to incorporate future datasets and extended for predictive analysis.
